Suggested Read — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 24, 2026

Suggested Read does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal information. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no network connections. Everything the app does happens entirely on your device.

Camera

The app uses your iPhone's camera to display a live augmented-reality view of the putting surface so it can show a suggested practice line. The camera feed is used only on screen, in real time. Images and video from the camera are not recorded, saved, or sent anywhere.

Information we collect

None. The app does not collect personal data, location data, contacts, photos, usage analytics, or device identifiers. Because the app has no servers and makes no network requests, no information ever leaves your device.

Third parties

The app does not include third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking software, and does not share any data with third parties.

Children's privacy

The app does not collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a future version, the updated policy will be posted at this same location with a new effective date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact: stableventures.apps@outlook.com

Support

Need help with Suggested Read, or have feedback? Email stableventures.apps@outlook.com and we'll get back to you. This page also serves as the app's support page.

Suggested Read is a practice aid. Green conditions, speed, grain, and stroke quality may affect actual ball roll. It is for practice use only.
